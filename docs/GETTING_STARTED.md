## 1. Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit SafeNode** at `https://app.safenode.app` (or your deployment URL)
2. **Click "Sign Up"** on the landing page
3. **Enter your email** and create a strong password
4. **Verify your email** (check your inbox for a verification link)
5. **Log in** with your credentials

### First Login

After logging in, you'll need to:
- Set up your master password (this encrypts your vault)
- Choose whether to enable biometric unlock
- Optionally set up a PIN for quick unlock

## 2. Master Password

### What is a Master Password?

Your **master password** is the key to your encrypted vault. It:
- Never leaves your device (zero-knowledge encryption)
- Is required to unlock your vault
- Should be strong and unique

### Creating a Strong Master Password

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Use at least 16 characters
- ‚úÖ Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- ‚úÖ Use a passphrase: `Correct-Horse-Battery-Staple-2024!`
- ‚úÖ Store it securely offline (password manager backup)

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use personal information (name, birthday, pet name)
- ‚ùå Reuse passwords from other accounts
- ‚ùå Write it down in an insecure place

### Unlocking Your Vault

Each time you access SafeNode, you'll need to:
1. Enter your master password
2. Or use biometric unlock (if enabled)
3. Or use your PIN (if set up)

**Note**: If you forget your master password, **we cannot recover it**. Your vault is encrypted with zero-knowledge, meaning we have no way to decrypt your data.

## 4. Syncing Across Devices

### How Sync Works

SafeNode uses **end-to-end encryption** for syncing:
- Your vault is encrypted before leaving your device
- Only you can decrypt it with your master password
- Changes sync automatically across all your devices

### Supported Platforms

- ‚úÖ **Web** - Access from any browser
- ‚úÖ **Desktop** - macOS, Windows, Linux apps
- ‚úÖ **Mobile** - iOS and Android apps
- ‚úÖ **Browser Extensions** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge

### Setting Up on Multiple Devices

1. **Install SafeNode** on your new device
2. **Log in** with the same account
3. **Enter your master password** to unlock
4. **Wait for sync** (vault will download automatically)

**Note**: All devices must use the same master password to decrypt the vault.

## 5. Security Features

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security:

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
5. **Save backup codes** (store securely offline)

**Login with 2FA:**
1. Enter your email and password
2. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
3. Access granted!

### Biometric Unlock

**Mobile (iOS/Android):**
- Enable Face ID or Touch ID in Settings
- Unlock with your biometrics instead of typing your master password

**Desktop:**
- macOS: Touch ID or Face ID
- Windows: Windows Hello
- Linux: Fingerprint reader (if configured)

### PIN Setup

For quick unlock:
1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. Click **Set PIN**
3. Enter a 4-8 digit PIN
4. Confirm your PIN
5. Use PIN for quick unlock (full security after device restart)

## 6. Tips & Best Practices

### Password Organization

- **Use tags** to organize entries (work, personal, finance, etc.)
- **Create folders/categories** for different types of entries
- **Add notes** with additional information (security questions, recovery emails)

### Security Best Practices

1. **Never share your master password** with anyone
2. **Use unique passwords** for every account
3. **Enable 2FA** on important accounts
4. **Regularly review** your vault entries
5. **Export backups** periodically (Settings ‚Üí Export)

### Vault Backup

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Export**
2. Enter your master password
3. Download the encrypted vault file
4. Store securely offline (encrypted USB drive, etc.)

**To Restore:**
1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Import**
2. Select your backup file
3. Enter your master password
4. Confirm import

### Password Health

Check your password health:
- Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Password Health**
- Review:
  - Weak passwords
  - Reused passwords
  - Compromised passwords (via breach monitoring)
- Update weak passwords using the generator
