# SafeNode Brand Kit

## Brand Mission

SafeNode exists to give everyone complete control over their digital security. We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, and that password management should be simple, secure, and accessible to all. Our mission is to protect your digital life with military-grade encryption while maintaining an elegant, intuitive user experience.

## SafeNode Story Overview

SafeNode was born from a simple frustration: existing password managers were either too complex, too expensive, or too insecure. We set out to build a solution that combines military-grade encryption with an elegant, intuitive interface.

Our zero-knowledge architecture means we can't see your passwords‚Äîeven if we wanted to. This isn't just a feature; it's our fundamental design principle. We believe that true security comes from giving users complete control over their data.

Today, SafeNode protects over 50,000 users worldwide, from individual consumers to enterprise teams. We're constantly evolving, but our core mission remains unchanged: make world-class security accessible to everyone.

## Visual Branding

### Logo Usage Guidelines

#### Minimum Size
- **Web**: 24px height minimum
- **Mobile**: 20px height minimum
- **Print**: 0.5 inch height minimum

#### Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to 2x the logo height on all sides.

#### Backgrounds
- **Light Mode**: Use full-color logo on white/light backgrounds
- **Dark Mode**: Use inverted logo on dark backgrounds
- **Gradient Backgrounds**: Use white logo variant

#### Don'ts
- ‚ùå Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ‚ùå Don't rotate the logo
- ‚ùå Don't change logo colors (except for dark mode inversion)
- ‚ùå Don't place logo on busy backgrounds without proper contrast
- ‚ùå Don't use logo smaller than minimum size

### Dark Mode vs Light Mode Branding

#### Light Mode
- **Background**: White (`#ffffff`)
- **Surface**: Light gray (`#f8fafc`)
- **Text**: Dark gray (`#1e293b`)
- **Accents**: Full-color brand purple/pink
- **Shadows**: Subtle gray shadows

#### Dark Mode
- **Background**: Dark slate (`#0f172a`)
- **Surface**: Slate (`#1e293b`)
- **Text**: Light gray (`#f1f5f9`)
- **Accents**: Lighter brand purple/pink tints
- **Shadows**: Darker, more pronounced shadows

### Background Textures & Patterns

#### Subtle Gradient Overlay
Use a subtle gradient overlay on hero sections:
```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(147, 51, 234, 0.05) 0%, rgba(236, 72, 153, 0.05) 100%);
```
